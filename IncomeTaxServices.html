Income Tax Basics: Who Should File ITR?: Filing an ITR is mandatory under certain circumstances. If your gross total income exceeds the basic exemption limit, you need to file ITR.
Exemption Limits:
  • Individuals below 60 years: ₹2.5 lakh
  • Individuals above 60 years but below 80 years: ₹3.0 lakh
  • Individuals above 80 years: ₹5.0 lakh

    • ITR Forms:
    Various ITR forms cater to different types of taxpayers:
    • ITR-1: For individuals with salary income, house property, and other sources.
    • ITR-2: For individuals with capital gains or foreign income.
    • ITR-3: For business owners and professionals.
    • ITR-4: For presumptive income from business or profession.
    • ITR-5: For firms, LLPs, AOPs, BOIs, etc.
    • ITR-6: For companies.
    • ITR-7: For persons with specific income sources32.
    Tax Rates:
    Individuals pay tax based on their income slab: Below ₹2,50,000: No tax. New Tax Regime (5% to 30%): Varies based on income.
